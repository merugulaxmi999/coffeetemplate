Coffee preparation is the process of tuming coffee beans into a bevarage.While the particular steps vary with the type of coffee and with the raw materials.the process includes four basic steps:raw coffee beans must beroasted ,the roasted coffee beans must then be ground.the ground coffee must then be mixed with hot water for a certain time(brewed).and finally the liquid coffee must be seperated from the used grounds.

Coffee is usually brewed immediately before drinking in most areas ,coffee may be purchased unprocessed,or already roasted,or already roasted and ground. Coffee is often vacuum packed to prevent oxidation and lengthen its shelf life.
